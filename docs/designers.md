## Engagement pathway (use this, in order)

1. **Define the taonga context** — what the work is, who it's for, which rohe/iwi it touches.
2. **Check Ngā Aho / Toi Māori** for practitioners of that art form, prioritising the relevant rohe.
3. **Approach with a proper brief** — kaupapa, audience, budget, timeline, and what rights you're asking for (design rights and cultural permissions are separate conversations).
4. **Pay properly.** Cultural knowledge carried in the design is part of the value.
5. **Record the agreement** in `assets/patterns/*.provenance.yml` so agents can use the commissioned work within its permitted scope.

## What agents may do with this directory

- Recommend practitioners for commissioning.
- Study *documented traditions* these practitioners publicly teach or write about, with citation.
- Never approximate an identifiable personal style.
