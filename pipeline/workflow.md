# Pipeline — Te Awa o te Mahi

The mandatory order for every piece of work. No stage may be skipped; the human gate is the point of the whole system.

```
0. BRIEF INTAKE
   Kaupapa, audience, context, rohe/iwi touched, deadline.
   → If brief names a living artist's style: stop, recommend commissioning (docs/designers.md).

1. SPECIALIST DESIGN
   Route to the right kaitoi agent(s): kowhaiwhai · tukutuku · whakairo · tokotoko ·
   raranga-taniko · toi-hou. (ta-moko never designs — knowledge/review only.)
   Parallel layers allowed; toi-hou integrates composites.
   Output: artwork layer(s) + rationale + significance declaration each.

2. SIGNIFICANCE LAYER (kaitiaki-tikanga)
   Full audit: meaning, attribution, zone, moko screen, artist screen, provenance, context.
   FAIL → back to step 1 with required fixes.

3. REVIEW WHĀNAU — first round
   kaumatua-reviewer · kuia-reviewer · whaea-reviewer · matua-reviewer · koro-reviewer,
   in parallel, each producing findings (BLOCKING / MUST-DISCUSS / ADVISORY).

4. DESIGNER RE-CRITIQUE
   Original specialist agents respond to every finding: fix, contest with reasons, or escalate.
   BLOCKING items must be fixed or escalated — never waved through.
   Loop 3↔4 up to two rounds; unresolved BLOCKING after two rounds → work pauses for human direction.

5. REVIEW PACK ASSEMBLY
   kaumatua-reviewer chairs; agents/review/review-pack-template.md is filled completely.

6. HUMAN GATE  ◄— the only approval that counts
   Real kaumātua / knowledge holders receive the pack.
   Approve → deliver. Change → back to step 1. Decline → work stops, no appeal.

7. RECORD
   Decision, conditions, and provenance updates recorded; lessons fed back into docs/.
```

## Service levels
- **Quick digital pieces** (broader-zone motifs only, internal use): steps 2–5 may run same-day, but the human gate still applies before external release.
- **Taonga-grade work** (marae, civic, personal whakapapa content): expect multiple human-gate rounds; build engagement time into the project plan from day one.
